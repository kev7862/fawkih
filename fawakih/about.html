About Us

Fawakih Import and Export Ltd is a Kenyan Company that deals with the Import and Export of Horticultural Produce. Fawakih Ltd origins lie in farming. This is why we understand our business so well. We believe in personal attention: it is this background that drives us to supply your customers and their families only the highest quality produce—all from carefully selected growers from almost every country in the world. As our current customers have found—and these include some of the biggest names in retail and hospitality—we are true to our promise in word and deed.
